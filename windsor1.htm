Windsor

17 h Hanoverian Stallion, 1992
by Wendland (Wendekreis/Göetz) out of Walburgis (Weitblick/Busoni XX)


Click here for a video of Windsor.

Click here for images of offspring Windsor

Windsor is by Wendland, who only stood at stud in Germany for three years but is listed by the breeding value index as number 120 out of more than 1800 stallions in his age group.  Wendland is sire of the currently competing Grand Prix dressage horse, Watch Me, ridden by Michael Poulan.   Wendland is by Wendekreis who is sire of 45 approved sons and has offspring competing in Grand Prix jumping and Grand Prix dressage.

Windsor is out of Walburgis by Weitblick who is sire of Grand Prix horses such at the jumper, Wotan.  Weitblick is by Fernjaeger who is in the pedigree of the International Grand Prix jumper stallion, For Pleasure, and is grandsire of International Grand Prix jumper and Olympic gold medalist in Montreal 1970 , Warwick Rex.
Click  for larger view

Windsor's grande dam is Bunette, by Busoni xx, who has an enormous influence in his pedigree.  Bunette produced the double Olympic gold medalist, Gigolo, ridden by Isabel Werth.  Busoni xx is ranked 17th with the breeding value index for dressage out of 1800 stallions in his age group.

Windsor is a powerful stallion with elegant movement and natural self-carriage, who scored an 8 on impulsion and elasticity for his trot. 

Windsor is an extraordinarily beautiful freshman stallion who is well suited for any performance career.
